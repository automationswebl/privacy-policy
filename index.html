Privacy Policy for N8n Automation Testing

Last Updated: 07/15/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how N8n Automation Testing ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Facebook and Instagram automation application. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Facebook and Instagram Data

When you connect our app to your Facebook and/or Instagram accounts, we may access:

  • Your Facebook posts and their metadata (text, images, timestamps)
  • Your Instagram posts and their metadata (images, videos, captions, hashtags)
  • Your Facebook and Instagram profile information (name, profile picture, basic demographics)
  • Your Facebook Pages and connected Instagram Business accounts (if you manage any)
  • Post engagement data (likes, comments, shares, views)
  • Publishing permissions for automated posting on both platforms
  • Instagram Stories and their metadata (when applicable)

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our app:

  • Authentication tokens and access permissions
  • Automation workflow configurations
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • API request logs for debugging purposes

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • Access times and dates

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Functions

We use your data to:

  • Execute automated posting workflows via n8n and similar automation platforms
  • Schedule and publish content to your Facebook account/pages and Instagram accounts
  • Cross-post content between Facebook and Instagram
  • Monitor and analyze post performance across both platforms
  • Provide automation testing and validation services
  • Manage hashtags and optimize content for Instagram

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Debug and troubleshoot automation workflows
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

3.3 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with Facebook's and Instagram's Platform Policies and Terms of Service
  • Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • Protect our rights and prevent misuse of our service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • Post data is temporarily cached for automation purposes
  • Workflow configurations are stored in our secure databases
  • Most data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Media files (images/videos) are temporarily processed for posting

4.2 Data Retention

  • Authentication tokens: Stored until you revoke access
  • Post data: Temporarily cached for up to 30 days
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging
  • Account information: Retained while your account is active
  • Media files: Temporarily stored during processing, then deleted

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • n8n: For automation workflow execution
  • Facebook Graph API: For accessing and posting to Facebook
  • Instagram Graph API: For accessing and posting to Instagram
  • Cloud hosting providers: For secure data storage and processing

5.2 No Data Selling

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Protect user safety

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your data that we have collected
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app permissions at any time

6.2 Facebook and Instagram Controls

You can manage our app's access through:

  • Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Instagram Settings → Apps and Websites (managed through Facebook)
  • Revoking specific permissions
  • Completely removing our app access

6.3 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us.

7. Facebook and Instagram Platform Compliance

Our app complies with both Facebook's and Instagram's Platform Policies, including:

  • Proper handling of user data and permissions
